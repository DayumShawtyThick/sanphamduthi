Tutorial (level 3 & 4)

- Level 3:

Players use 2 keys A, D, S, W to move the character horizontal, up and down.

Players move characters to leaked locations water, lock the water valve to save water. 10 pipes closed equal winning

If the player loses, return to Level 1 again.

- Level 4:

Players use 2 keys A and D to move the character to touch the dirt to plant trees

When tree appears, CO2 and O2 smoke will blow up.

During 90 seconds, if the player don't plant enough 8 trees, you will return to Level 1 and play from the beginning.

In 120 seconds, the player with 10 points wins and gets to the Win room.
