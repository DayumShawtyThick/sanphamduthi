Tutorial (level 1 & 2)

- Level 1:

Players use 2 keys A and D to move the character to touch the balls to turn off those devices

For each light bulb off, the player gets 01 point. In 120 seconds, the player with 5 points wins and is allowed to play Level 2.

If the player loses, return to Level 1 again.

- Level 2:

Players use 2 keys A and D to move the character to touch the balls to turn off those devices

When giant spiders appear, the player left click to shoot to destroy the giant spider.

For every light that goes out or the giant spider is destroyed, the player gets 01 point

In 120 seconds, the player with 10 points wins and gets to the Win room.
